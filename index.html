About

We are an independently-owned custom guitar effects and equipment shop based in Hornell, NY.

For a few years now, we've been building custom pedals and clones, doing pedal mods and repairs, wiring up custom cables, doing pedal board setups, and modding/installing guitar electronics.

We create custom effects pedals, pedal clones, guitar/pedal mods, instrument and patch cables, and custom pedalboard setups.

Our wares are of the highest quality. Our professionalism is unmatched. Our attention to detail is awe-inspiring.

You were born to find these pedals. The day has come.
